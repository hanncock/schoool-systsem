Apps to Download

We require our students to download the following apps from the Play store:


1.Microsoft Teams – To be used for notes, assignments, classroom interaction between students and to the teacher.


2.Google Meet – To be used for live class sessions and revisions.You will receive a document via WhatsApp explaining how to Log into the platform. Our IT Technician will be available to guide you henceforth.

Prerequisits

The platform majorly requires of the students to have the following:


1.Smartphone / Tablet/ Laptop or a Desktop with a webcam. A webcam is a requiremnent in desktop computers for online live teacher to students class sessions. Therefore students will be able to engage the teacher one-on-one .


2.A Stable Internet connection. We recommend an excellent network for devices using bundles.


Payment

A payment of KES. 2500/= per student per month was agreed upon to facilitate internet access and paying the teachers involved.


After payment you will immediately receive an email address and a password to sign-in the platform and access content together with the class sessions.


For requests on payment kindly contact via 0797967370 or Class teacher for Fairmonts International School.
